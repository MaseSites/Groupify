Questions & answers

What is Groupify?

Groupify is a simple, modern platform that connects people who share the same interests. Create your own activity or join one nearby — browse the map, see who else is going, and take it from the group chat into real life.

What does “Groupify” actually mean?

It's the English words group and unify smushed together: get together as a group and have a great time — that's the whole idea. #findyourpeople

What does Groupify cost?

Nothing. Groupify is run with a lot of heart, so the app and all of its features are free. Some hosts may charge for their own activities — a barista course needs beans, after all.

Where can I download Groupify?

On the Apple App Store and on Google Play — just search for “Groupify”, or use the download buttons below.

Which languages does Groupify speak?

Groupify is currently available in German and English.

Where is Groupify developed?

Groupify is run by groupify GmbH in Zürich and engineered together with sonono GmbH in Basel — 100% Swiss made.

How do you keep members and activities legit?

New members go through a two-step sign-up, and every activity is reviewed and filtered. All data is stored in Switzerland (with Cyon in Basel), so your club — and your data — stay in good hands.
